About Higher Love

Higher Love is an invite-only gathering for accomplished gay founders and executives. It's a space where you can be fully yourself—where ambition, authenticity, and community intersect.

For too long, successful gay men have had to compartmentalize who they are. We created Higher Love to change that. This isn't just another retreat. It's a brotherhood of accomplished men who get it.
