Higher Love

Higher Love is born from a simple belief: there should be a space for gay men—especially those building companies and leading teams—to be fully themselves.

We believe that gay founders and executives don't need to compartmentalize. They deserve a room that sees and celebrates all of who they are: the ambitious founder, the visionary leader, the loyal friend, the devoted partner, the creative spirit.

For three days in September, we're gathering accomplished gay men who understand that business excellence and authentic self-expression aren't mutually exclusive—they're deeply connected.
