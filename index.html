What is Higher Love?

Higher Love is an exclusive three-day gathering in September 2026 in the Texas Hill Country, designed for accomplished gay founders and executives who are ready to show up as their whole authentic selves.

This is not another networking event focused on business cards and surface-level conversations. Higher Love creates space for meaningful connection—where the complexity of being a gay man in leadership can be explored, celebrated, and supported without shame or pretense.

Together, we'll share experiences, challenge assumptions, and forge genuine friendships rooted in mutual respect and authentic understanding.

A Letter From Re Perez

For years, I've built companies, scaled teams, and achieved what society calls "success." But underneath these accomplishments was a quiet struggle—the emotional exhaustion of constantly calibrating who I could be in professional spaces.

I would toggle between versions of myself: the visionary founder in investor meetings, the composed executive with my team, the strategic thinker in board rooms. Each version was real, but the constant switching took a profound toll.

The emotional complexity of being a gay man in leadership is rarely discussed. We navigate unique pressures—questions about our authenticity, our leadership style, our place at the table. We wonder if our success is fully ours or if we've had to shrink parts of ourselves to fit the mold.

What if there was a space where you didn't have to choose? Where you could bring all of yourself—your ambition AND your vulnerability, your professional excellence AND your humanity, your boardroom brilliance AND your queerness?

That's what Higher Love is. A room where the whole gay man is welcome. Where we celebrate not just what we've built, but who we've become in the process. Where the brotherhood goes beyond business—it's about genuine, soul-level connection.

I'm inviting you to join us.

With warmth,
Re

Frequently Asked Questions

Who Should Apply? +

Higher Love is designed for accomplished gay men who are founders, C-suite executives, or senior leaders in their organizations. We're looking for people who are successful in their careers and ready to deepen their connections with a community of peers who truly understand the unique complexities of gay leadership.

What is the Cost? +

Pricing will be announced upon application review. The cost includes three nights accommodation, all meals, activities, and world-class hospitality in the Texas Hill Country.

Is This Only For Out Individuals? +

Higher Love is a confidential community. We welcome all accomplished gay men, regardless of where they are in their journey of being open about their identity. Your privacy and safety are paramount.

Can I Bring a Plus One? +

Higher Love is designed as a single-gender gathering to create a safe, focused space. This is intentional—it allows everyone to show up authentically without additional considerations.

How are Attendees Selected? +

We carefully curate the community to ensure diverse perspectives, industries, and experiences. Applications are reviewed based on professional accomplishment, readiness for authentic connection, and fit with our community values.

Ready to Join the Brotherhood?

Express your interest in attending Higher Love. We'll be in touch with details about the application process and what comes next.
