Stop Being the Only One in the Room

Higher Love Summit is an invitation-only 3.5-day luxury summit designed for accomplished gay founders, entrepreneurs, and executives who are building billion-dollar companies, leading teams, and making impact in their industries.

This is a safe space where you can be fully yourself—dropping the mask you wear in boardrooms and on stage. Connect with peers who understand the unique experience of building extraordinary things while navigating identity, authenticity, and leadership.

What to Expect

Intimate Gatherings

Small group discussions with founders, executives, and world-class facilitators. Share real stories, real challenges, and real solutions with peers who get it.

Transformative Workshops

Learn from thought leaders including Joe Dispenza on consciousness and innovation. Explore leadership, authenticity, spirituality, and the psychology of success.

Luxury Venue

Stunning Texas Hill Country setting designed for rest, reflection, and connection. World-class accommodations, gourmet meals, and wellness activities included.

Peer Network

Build lasting relationships with 21 other accomplished gay founders and executives. Many of these connections will become lifelong friendships and business partnerships.

Authentic Leadership

Explore how to lead with authenticity and integrity. Develop strategies to build cultures where your teams can be fully themselves, driving innovation and loyalty.

Vision & Legacy

Clarify your vision, values, and legacy. Connect your business goals with your deeper purpose and create actionable strategies for the next chapter of your life.

Our Values

Higher Love Summit is built on principles of authenticity, excellence, and community. We believe in the power of gathering accomplished leaders in service of something greater.

Authenticity

Be fully yourself. Drop the masks and corporate armor. Authentic connection creates real impact and lasting change.

Excellence

We gather only the most accomplished founders and executives. This is a brotherhood of extraordinary builders.

Founding Circle

22 seats for the first cohort. This intimate group creates deep bonds and meaningful partnerships that will last a lifetime.

Integrity

What's shared here stays here. We hold confidentiality sacred and create a container for vulnerability and growth.

Vitality

We prioritize health, wellness, and joy. Exceptional leaders take care of their minds, bodies, and spirits.

Community

No one should build alone. Together, we create a powerful brotherhood of support, accountability, and shared success.

Who Should Apply?

Higher Love Summit is designed for accomplished gay founders and executives who meet these criteria:

You Should Apply If You:

  • ✓ Building or leading a company with significant revenue, team, or impact
  • ✓ Openly gay or in the LGBTQ+ community and identifying as gay
  • ✓ Ready for transformation and open to deep conversations about authenticity and leadership
  • ✓ Committed to the full 3.5-day experience September 10-13, 2026
  • ✓ Aligned with our values of authenticity, excellence, and community

We Are Not the Right Fit If:

  • ✗ You're looking for a networking event or a traditional conference
  • ✗ You're not ready to explore vulnerability and personal transformation
  • ✗ You need concrete business solutions rather than inner work and leadership clarity
  • ✗ You view spirituality with skepticism or are uncomfortable exploring consciousness and interconnectedness
