Who I Am

And Why I'm
Building This

My name is Re Perez.

I came out in the early 1990s in New York City. And while it was safer then than in previous decades, I still had almost no gay role models who looked like what I wanted to become.

The openly gay men I could see publicly were either closeted in the professional world or fit a narrow stereotype that didn't reflect me. I didn't see a gay man who was serious, ambitious, spiritually curious, and building something of consequence.

So I became that man, largely without a map.

For 24 years I've been a brand consultant, leading transformations for founders, executives, and companies across industries. I'm a speaker and the author of Your Brand Should Be Gay (Even If You're Not).

I recently launched Category of One, a video podcast and YouTube channel exploring what it means to build a brand, and a life, that stands alone in its category.

In parallel, I've spent 24 years inside some of the most powerful personal development and transformation communities. Plant medicine ceremonies. High-vibe, conscious gatherings of entrepreneurs who weren't just financially successful, they were spiritually and emotionally evolved.

People living intentional lives. People using their businesses for impact and purpose.

I love all of it.

And I kept wondering: where are the other gay men in these rooms?

The answer, most of the time, was: I was the only one.

Higher Love is the room I kept waiting for someone else to build.

Qualifying

Is This Your Room?

This is not for the gay man who is still figuring out who he is.

This is for the man who knows, and is ready to stop apologizing for it with his presence.

This room was built for you if

  • You're a gay founder, entrepreneur, or executive who has built something real, a business, a career, a body of work that reflects who you actually are.
  • You've done the inner work. Landmark. Tony Robbins. Therapy. Plant medicine. You've sat with your shadows. You've done the programs, read the books, attended the seminars, and you know that the next layer isn't something you can access alone.
  • You're spiritually curious without being performative about it. You're drawn to consciousness, human design, plant medicine, the bigger questions, but you get quietly frustrated by the spiritual community's tendency toward surface and performance.
  • You're into health and your body. Fitness, optimization, maybe biohacking. The instrument matters to you.
  • You live an intentional life. Your calendar reflects your values, not just your obligations. Who you spend time with is a deliberate choice.
  • You get bored by surface conversation. You are not interested in what the weather is like. You want the conversation that shifts something.
  • You love business but you're building from a different place now, from joy, from purpose, from mission, or you're actively seeking how to make that transition.
  • At times, you don't feel like you fully fit in gay culture. You've always found yourself in rooms that were mostly not gay, not out of shame, but because those were the rooms being built at the level you were operating.
  • You are ready, genuinely ready, to be in a room where you are not the only one.

Equally Important

This room is not for you if

  • You're coming to network, pitch, or grow your contact list. Higher Love has no infrastructure for that.
  • You want the credential without the depth, the presence in the room without the willingness to be present inside it.
  • You are not yet ready to let the performance go, even for 3.5 days.
