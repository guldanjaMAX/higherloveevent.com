What is Higher Love Summit?

Curated Brotherhood

Higher Love Summit is a carefully curated retreat for gay founders, entrepreneurs, and executives who are building and leading meaningful ventures. This is a space where you're not the only one—where your experience as a gay leader is understood, celebrated, and leveraged as a source of strength.

Beyond business discussions, we explore the unique leadership perspectives, challenges, and opportunities that come with being a gay founder or executive in today's entrepreneurial landscape.

This summit brings together 22 accomplished individuals for an intimate 3.5-day experience focused on genuine connection, strategic insights, and community building.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply to Higher Love Summit?

▼
We welcome gay founders, entrepreneurs, executives, investors, advisors, and mission-driven leaders who have demonstrated significant success in their ventures or roles. The key criteria are accomplishment, commitment to growth, and a genuine desire to connect authentically with peers.

When does the application process close?

▼
Applications will be reviewed on a rolling basis until all 22 seats are filled. We encourage early application as spots are expected to fill quickly. Final application deadline will be announced as we approach the event date.

What's included in the summit investment?

▼
The investment includes all accommodations, meals, beverages, workshop materials, activities, and ground transportation in Austin. It covers the entire 3.5-day experience. Pricing will be shared with applicants upon application review.

Are scholarships available?

▼
A limited number of partial scholarships may be available for exceptional candidates who demonstrate financial need and strong alignment with the summit's mission. Please note your interest in your application if relevant.

What is the expected group size?

▼
We are limiting the summit to exactly 22 founding members. This intimate size ensures meaningful connections, authentic conversations, and personalized experiences for every participant.

What's the travel situation?

▼
Participants are responsible for flights to Austin. Ground transportation from the airport to the venue is included. We recommend arriving a day early to adjust and explore Austin.

Can I bring a partner or guest?

▼
The summit is designed as a peer-focused retreat for the 22 participants. Partners and guests are not included in this inaugural event. However, we're exploring companion experiences for future summits based on interest.

What if I can't make September dates?

▼
We understand scheduling challenges. If selected, we'll work with you on potential date accommodations if possible. We're also planning future Higher Love Summit events, so stay tuned.

Who is organizing this?

▼
Higher Love Summit is founded and curated by Re Perez, a serial entrepreneur and gay business leader committed to building authentic communities where gay founders and executives can thrive together.
