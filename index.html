Why This Matters

Success in business often demands we become less of ourselves. We learn to code-switch, to hide parts of our identity, to fit into rooms not designed for us. 70% is not enough. We deserve spaces where being ourselves is not a liability, but a strength.

Higher Love is an invitation to step into your full power—as a founder, as an executive, and as a gay man. No apologies. No shrinking. Just authentic connection with peers who understand the unique intersections of your journey.

About This Gathering

Higher Love was created by Re Perez, a serial entrepreneur and advocate for authentic leadership. The vision is simple: create a space where accomplished gay men in business can show up fully—without the burden of code-switching or hiding parts of themselves.

This is not a typical conference. This is a retreat designed for depth, authenticity, and meaningful connection among peers who are redefining what leadership looks like.
