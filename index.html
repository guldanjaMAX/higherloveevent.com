What is Higher Love?

For accomplished gay men, there's often an invisible pressure to navigate the world differently in different rooms. Your leadership room. Your family room. Your community room. Never quite all of you.

Higher Love is designed for you. A curated gathering of 22 accomplished gay founders, entrepreneurs, and executives. Three days in the Texas Hill Country where you can be entirely yourself. Where you can lead fully. Where you can trust deeply.

This is not a conference. Not a retreat with breakout sessions. Higher Love is an experience designed by Re Perez specifically for the evolved gay man who has built something of meaning and wants to build community with peers who understand.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can apply?

We welcome applications from accomplished gay founders, entrepreneurs, and executives. If you've built something of meaning and you're interested in being part of an intentional community with peers, we'd love to hear from you.

What if I don't consider myself a "founder"?

We use "founder" loosely. If you've founded a company, a movement, a nonprofit, or even a meaningful practice within a larger organization, you're welcome. The common thread is building something of value.

Is this a business networking event?

Higher Love will naturally result in meaningful professional connections. But it's not designed as a networking event. It's designed as a place where you can be fully yourself with peers who get it.

What's the cancellation policy?

More than 90 days before: Full refund minus $500 admin fee. 60-90 days before: 50% refund. Less than 60 days: No refund, but you can transfer your seat to another approved attendee. See Terms & Conditions for full details.

What about photography?

Professional photography may be taken for promotional purposes. If you prefer not to be photographed, just let us know when you're accepted, and we'll respect your request.

Do you have payment plans?

Yes. For those accepted at the founding rate ($7,500), we offer three monthly installments of $2,500 each.
