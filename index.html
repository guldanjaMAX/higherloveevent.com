A Brotherhood for Accomplished Gay Men

September 17-20, 2026 | Austin, Texas

Finally, a room designed for the whole gay man.

If you've built something meaningful in business and are looking for a peer group that actually gets it—both the professional caliber and the lived experience—Higher Love is for you.

This is a gathering for accomplished gay founders, executives, investors, and operators. People who are tired of being the only one in the room. People who want to lead from their whole selves.

The Premise

Gay men at the highest levels of business have often had to code-switch, compartmentalize, and perform. We've gotten really good at it. But the cost is real.

Higher Love is three days designed around the full intersection of who you are: your ambition, your identity, your integrity, and your humanity. No code switching. No performing. Just the whole version of you with other whole versions.

What to Expect

This isn't a typical conference. Each day is designed around a different theme:

  • Day 1 (Welcome Reception): September 17 evening—arrival, welcome reception, and opening circle
  • Day 2: September 18—curated roundtables, peer advisory sessions, morning movement, Hill Country excursion
  • Day 3: September 19—facilitated deep dives, creative expression, wellness, fireside conversations
  • Day 4 (Closing): September 20—closing circle, breakfast, final reflections

You'll spend time in small groups, one-on-one conversations, and full gatherings. Think structured peer advisory mixed with open conversation, wellness, and shared experiences in a beautiful setting in the Texas Hill Country.

Who's Invited

We're looking for accomplished gay men who are:

  • Founders, executives, investors, or operators
  • Building or leading something meaningful
  • Interested in peer connection and authentic community
  • Open to vulnerability and growth
  • Committed to showing up fully for three days

You don't need to be out in every setting, but you do need to be honest about who you are and willing to engage authentically.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Higher Love for?

Higher Love is for accomplished gay men who have built something meaningful in business and are looking for a peer group that matches both their professional caliber and their lived experience. Founders, executives, investors, and partners who are tired of being the only one in the room.

Can I bring my partner or spouse?

This gathering is designed as a space for gay men to connect with each other. The conversations, exercises, and experiences are built around the shared identity of being a gay man who leads. Austin is an incredible city and many attendees may choose to extend their trip with their partner before or after.

What will we actually do over three days?

Each day is designed around a different theme, blending structured peer advisory sessions with open conversation, wellness, and shared experiences. Curated roundtables, facilitated deep dives, morning movement and breathwork, creative expression, Hill Country excursions, and evening firepits.

What makes this different from other retreats?

Higher Love is designed from the ground up for accomplished gay men. The conversations, programming, and peer dynamics are all built around the specific intersection of identity, ambition, and lived experience. No code switching. No performing. Just the full version of who you are.

What is the refund policy?

If you attend the full gathering and genuinely feel it did not deliver on what was promised, contact Re within 7 days of the event and you will receive a full refund. No hoops. No fine print.

How do I apply?

Email re@higherloveevent.com with a brief note about yourself, your background, and why you're interested in Higher Love. We'll be in touch to learn more about your fit for the gathering.

What's included in the ticket price?

Everything—three nights accommodation, all meals, all programming, and all experiences. Your only cost is getting to Austin. We're covering everything else so you can focus entirely on the experience and the community.

Is this about business networking?

Not primarily. While you'll absolutely meet accomplished people and make meaningful connections, this is not structured as a networking event. It's about authentic community, peer support, and the integration of identity and leadership. Business friendships often emerge naturally from that kind of real connection.

What if I'm not out at work?

You don't need to be out in every area of your life to benefit from Higher Love. You do need to be honest about who you are and willing to engage authentically with the group. What's shared at Higher Love stays at Higher Love.

How many people will be there?

We're limiting this to 40-50 people. Large enough for real community, small enough for genuine depth and connection.
