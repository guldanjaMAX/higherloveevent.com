The Experience

Three Days.
One Room That Changes Everything.

By the second day, something shifts. You stop editing yourself. You stop scanning the room. You just... exhale. That is what three days in this room does. A private luxury venue in Austin, Texas, September 18–20, 2026. Every conversation, every late night firepit, every quiet morning is designed around what actually matters to you.

By the third morning, something will have shifted. You won’t be able to name it yet. But you’ll notice that you stopped editing yourself sometime on day two, and it didn’t cost you anything. In fact, it gave you something back that you forgot you had.
