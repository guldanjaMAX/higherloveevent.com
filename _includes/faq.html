Frequently Asked

Higher Love is for accomplished gay men who have built something meaningful in business and are looking for a peer group that matches both their professional caliber and their lived experience. Founders, executives, investors, and partners who are tired of being the only one in the room or the only gay man at the table. If you have spent years succeeding in rooms that required you to edit yourself, this gathering was designed for you.

This gathering is designed as a space for gay men to connect with each other without the filter of being someone's partner or plus one. The conversations, exercises, and experiences are built around the shared identity of being a gay man who leads. That said, Austin is an incredible city. Many attendees may choose to extend their trip and bring their partner before or after the gathering. Re is happy to share local recommendations.

Each day is designed around a different theme, blending structured peer advisory sessions with open conversation, wellness, and shared experiences. Think curated roundtables, facilitated deep dives, morning movement and breathwork, creative expression, local excursions, and evening firepits. This is not a conference with panels and name tags. It is an immersive experience where every man shows up fully and leave transformed. The full schedule will be shared with accepted members.

Most retreats and masterminds are designed for a general audience where you happen to be welcome. Higher Love is designed from the ground up for accomplished gay men. The conversations, the programming, and the peer dynamics are all built around the specific intersection of identity, ambition, and lived experience that the men in this room share. There is no code switching. No explaining yourself. No performing. Just the full version of who you are in a room with others who understand because they have lived it too.

This is an intimate gathering by design. The founding cohort will be limited to a small group of men, likely between 15 and 25. This is not a conference with hundreds of people. Every man in the room will be personally vetted and every conversation will be personal. The size is intentional so that by the time you leave, there are no strangers.

After you submit your application, Re Perez will personally review it. If there is a potential fit, you will be invited to a one on one conversation with Re. This is not a sales call. It is a genuine exploration of whether this gathering is right for you and whether you are right for the room. If it is a mutual yes, you will receive your invitation to join the founding cohort.
